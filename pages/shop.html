What We Sell

We have a small pet shop that primarily focuses on small animals and common reptiles. We carry various dog supplies and minimal cat supplies.


We carry small live pets including guinea pigs, hamsters, parakeets and hermit crabs. We also carry a small selection of lizards and frogs. Always in stock are our feeders such as crickets and mice in different age stages and worms.


Please note** We DO NOT sell any fish or fish supplies.
